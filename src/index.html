About

Hi, I'm Matt, a freelance web developer with a passion for creating unique, functional, and visually stunning websites.


I graduated from the University of Guelph with a computer engineering degree, but soon realized that my true calling was to design and build websites that help businesses bring their brand to the web.


I take great joy in understanding my clients' needs and desires and translating that into a beautiful and functional website that truly represents the essence of their business.

From design to launch, I am dedicated to ensuring that each of my clients is thrilled with the final result.
